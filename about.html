About

Dmitry Chernysh

I am a lettering artist based in Melbourne. I help my cli­ents decorate their interiors and menus to attract new custo­mers and create cozy atmosphere.

I draw custom lettering for cafés, wed­dings, illust­ra­tions, logos, and packaging. I study and practice calli­graphy — it gives my letter­forms correct struc­ture, rhythm, and shape.

I work with pens, markers, and brushes on paper, walls and other mediums, but most of all I love drawing on chalk­boards. For my projects I use fine chalks that have vivid, saturated colours and don’t scratch the surface.
